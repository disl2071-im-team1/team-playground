Summary

What we learned

  • You can run a product team with AI doing the coordination layer
  • The constraint is not the AI's capability, it's your ability to structure work so AI can act on it
  • Modular teams and modular code reinforce each other
  • Token limits, ambiguity, and meeting-tool debates are still very human problems
The single most important line: the constraint is structure, not capability. If the audience remembers one thing, make it this.

If you want to try this

  • Build async-first: everything documented, nothing lost in calls

  • Review your how and why frequently
