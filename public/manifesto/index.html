MANIFESTO

Ten loud little rules we learned building a real product with AI as a teammate, not a tool.

  1. 1

    Trust is the foundation of everything.

    Without it, no tool, process, or plan will hold you together.

  2. 2

    Be brave enough to fully commit.

    Insight only lives in the deep end.

  3. 3

    Align before you act.

    Agree on why you're doing this, what you're building, and how everyone contributes.

  4. 4

    Make something real as fast as possible.

    People can react to a thing, but never to an idea.

  5. 5

    Never underestimate a well-timed dad joke.

    A team that laughs together ships together.

  6. 6

    Stop and reflect regularly.

    Use the ceremonies you already have. Retrospectives and check-ins exist for a reason.

  7. 7

    Document as you go.

    Undocumented work is forgotten work, and your future self will not thank you.

  8. 8

    Empower, don't control.

    Let the person closest to the work lead in that moment.

  9. 9

    Spend real time debating decisions.

    Then execute without second-guessing once the call is made.

  10. 10

    Stay critical of your choices and the AI's output.

    Comfort is not the same as correctness.
