Manifesto.

Ten principles we learned building a real product with AI as a teammate, not a tool.

  1. 01

    Trust is the foundation of everything. Without it, no tool, process, or plan will hold you together.

  2. 02

    Be brave enough to fully commit. Insight only lives in the deep end.

  3. 03

    Align before you act. Agree on why you're doing this, what you're building, and how everyone contributes.

  4. 04

    Make something real as fast as possible. People can react to a thing, but never to an idea.

  5. 05

    Never underestimate a well-timed dad joke. A team that laughs together ships together.

  6. 06

    Stop and reflect regularly. Use the ceremonies you already have. Retrospectives and check-ins exist for a reason.

  7. 07

    Document as you go. Undocumented work is forgotten work, and your future self will not thank you.

  8. 08

    Empower, don't control. Let the person closest to the work lead in that moment.

  9. 09

    Spend real time debating decisions. Then execute without second-guessing once the call is made.

  10. 10

    Stay critical of your choices and the AI's output. Comfort is not the same as correctness.
