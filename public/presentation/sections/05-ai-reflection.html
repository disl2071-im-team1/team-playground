A design choice Stilla made

  • Design moved faster than debate. Stilla and Claude collaborated on app design elements.
  • Token anxiety is real and forced a change. We ran out and had to install Claude Code to save tokens for coding.
  • The architecture emerged from team structure. We planned five people and got five tabs.

What didn't change: Humans still had to merge manually, keeping accountability in the loop.
