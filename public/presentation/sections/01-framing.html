Who it's for, and what it is

  • Municipality environmental departments
  • The need: officer-ready visibility of five hazards, air quality, bathing-water algae, fire risk, extreme heat, and rainfall
  • Clean Path: a monitoring and decision surface, one shell per hazard
  • Built and running on real, source-tagged data, every layer labelled measured, forecast, modelled or sampled
