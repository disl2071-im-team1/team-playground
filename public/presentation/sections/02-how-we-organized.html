How we organized

A headless team

  • No project manager, no team lead. Five equals working at the same time
  • Stilla supported the meetings: prepared agendas and discussion topics, summarised each session, and surfaced the open tickets
  • It did not run standups, assign the work, or own the backlog. We did that together
  • Everything stayed visible and explicit through GitHub PRs and Linear
Pelle. "Headless" means no PM role exists at all. The team self-organised as five equals. Stilla was a meeting aide, not a coordinator: it prepared agendas, summarised sessions, and surfaced tickets, but the team owned the standups, the assignments, and the backlog.
